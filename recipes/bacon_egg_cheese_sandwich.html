Bacon, Egg & Cheese Sandwich

Description

A breakfast classic, the bacon, egg, and cheese is an incredibly flavorful meal for any time of day. One of the key things I feel that is lacking is a dressing or sauce to bring it together. For this, I use the egg yolk by leaving it whole during cooking and breaking it in the sandwich.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need:

  • Thick Cut Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Cheese (choose one you like)
  • Bread (choose one you like
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

For this recipe you will need to:

  1. Starting from a cold pan, lay the Thick Cut Bacon in one layer
  2. Set the heat to medium and cook making sure to turn frequently.
  3. Once the Thick Cut Bacon is cooked, remove and drain most of the oil from the pan, but leave enough to coat the pan.
  4. Crack the Eggs on the pan.
  5. Season with Salt and Pepper to taste.
  6. Place Bread on the pan alongside Eggs to toast
  7. Cover the pan to ensure the yolk cooks but does not harden. We want to make sure the yolk can still run.
  8. Once Eggs are finshed, assemble the sandwich in the pan layering eggs, cheese, and then bacon.
  9. Remove the sandwich from the pan to a plate and press down to break the yolks.
  10. Cut in half or leave whole and enjoy.
