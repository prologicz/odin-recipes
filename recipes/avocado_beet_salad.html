Avocado Beet Salad

Description

This recipe was inspired by the "Avocado Matrix" in the book Salt Fat Acid Heat by Samin Nosrat. It brings together several distinct flavors into an incredible experience.

The book allows for different combinations of vegetables, dressings, and toppings but this is one of the simpler yet flavorful ones. Using spring mix, ripe avocados, roasted beets, some citrus (in this case oranges) and a lime vinaigrette, this salad makes a refreshing lunch. Remember to season every ingredient to get the best out of this salad.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need:

  • Avocados
  • Beets
  • Spring Mix
  • Oranges
  • Olive Oil
  • Limes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Vinegar

Steps

For the vinaigrette:

  1. Squeeze the Limes into a measuring cup
  2. Mix the Olive Oil and a dollop of Dijon Mustard
  3. Whisk, shake, and/or stir until it becomes a uniform mixture
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste

For the salad:

  1. Use 3/4 of the vinaigrette to coat the Spring Mix and lay on plate
  2. Cut the Beets into wedges and coat in Vinegar and salt. Place on top of Spring Mix
  3. Scoop Avocados by the spoonful and lightly coat each spoonful with vinaigrette and Salt. Place alongside Beets.
  4. Peel the Orange skin and cut into bite size wedges and sprinkle some Salt. Place alongside Beets and Avocados
  5. Finally feel free to add cheese, pepper, nuts, dried fruits or other toppings to make it your own.
